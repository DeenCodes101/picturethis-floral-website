Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our plants and services

Start with plants known for air purification and calming effects, such as Lavender, Jasmine, or Aloe Vera. Check our home page for recommendations, and visit the Plant Care Guide for detailed needs of each plant.

Every plant is carefully wrapped and secured in eco-friendly packaging to prevent damage during transit. Pots are stabilized and soil is covered to minimize spillage. Most orders arrive within 3–5 business days.

If your plant arrives damaged or unhealthy, contact us within 7 days at picturethis@floral.com with photos. We will send a replacement or issue a full refund — no questions asked.

Some plants, like English Ivy and Aloe Vera, can be toxic to pets if ingested. We label pet-safe options in our shop and recommend keeping plants out of reach of curious animals. Contact us if you need pet-friendly recommendations.

Watering depends on the plant type, season, and room humidity. As a general rule, let the top inch of soil dry before watering again. Our Plant Care Guide has specific instructions for every plant we sell.
